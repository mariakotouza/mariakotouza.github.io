Golden Paragraph

Ms Maria Th. Kotouza received her Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in July 2016. Currently she is pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the same Department at the Intelligent Systems & Software Engineering Labgroup (ISSEL), under the supervision of the professor Mr. Pericles Mitkas. Her research interests lie in the field of Software Engineering, Big Data Analytics, Data Mining and Machine Learning.

She has worked as a Software Engineer in the RAPP European Research Project from November 2016 till January 2017. She has also worked at the Centre of Research & Technology - Hellas (CERTH), at the Institute of Applied Biosciences (INAB), performing Big Data Analytics in gene sequence data. Currently, she is working as a Data and Software Analyst in National Research projects. Additionally, she is working as a teaching assistant in the Department courses: (a) Data Structures and (b) Object Oriented Programming. She is a member of the Technical Chamber of Greece and an Instructor at Software Carpentry
