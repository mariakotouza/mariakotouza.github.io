Hi! My name is Maria Kotouza, I am a Software & Data
Engineer and a PhD Student at Aristotle University of
Thessaloniki, Greece

I have a 4+ years’ experience in Software Engineering & Data Analytics, and I have participated in European and National research projects working with data coming from various scientific fields such as Robotics, Biology, Energy, Fashion. My research interests lie primarily in developing generative methods based on Machine Learning in order to support the analysis of high throughput and heterogeneous data originating from different sources. My motivation to develop these methods is to uncover relationships in big datasets, explore patterns and extract knowledge in order to help domain experts with the decision-making process.
